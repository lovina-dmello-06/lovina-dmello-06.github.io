Your LLM Stack Is a 2008 Database With Better Marketing

Why ML security is dominated by misconfiguration, not missing features. Drawing on a synthesis of 139 peer-reviewed papers on production ML, this talk shows how most real-world failures come down to basic infrastructure mistakes, like the ShadowRay incident where a default config left off exposed massive amounts of data, and why operational hygiene (secrets management, encryption, scanning) matters more than exotic defenses.
